Ajey Nagar

Ajey Nagar, better known as CarryMinati, is an Indian YouTuber and streamer from Faridabad, India. He is known for his comedic skits and reactions to various online topics on his channel CarryMinati. He has currently 28.5M Subscribers on CarryMinati YouTube Channel.

Ashish Chanchalani

Ashish Chanchalani is a comedian and YouTube personality and was passionate about acting since his childhood. The actor has become an internet sensation through his videos. He has currently 23.3M Subscribers on Ashish Chanchalani Vines YouTube Channel.

Amit Bhadana

Amit Bhadana is an Indian comedian and YouTube personality. In May 2020, he claimed to be the first Indian individual YouTube content creator to reach 20 million subscribers. He has currently 22.4M Subscribers on Amit Bhadana YouTube Channel.

Gaurav Chaudhary

Gaurav Chaudhary, known professionally as Technical Guruji, is an Indian YouTube Personality based in UAE. Gaurav is notable for producing YouTube videos concerning technology in Hindi. He has currently 20.6M Subscribers on Technical Guruji YouTube Channel.
